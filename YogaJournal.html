Project: Yoga Journal Magazine

Using the Yoga Journal brand and style guide as a starting point, I was responsible for conceptualizing, art directing, and designing the magazine's front of book, Om section, the Product/Style pages, as well as a number of other columns throughout the magazine.

Above are example spreads from Om, the Product/Style Pages, and the Home Practice column. With each issue I had the opportunity to collaborate with a number of talented photographers, stylists, and illustrators whose work is featured in the above spreads.

In order to advance the design of the magazine, and create the clean, approachable look and feel seen above, I regularly compiled mood boards, photo research, and magazine spread designs.
