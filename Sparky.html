WORK EXPERIENCE: Sparky: The Life and Times of Charles M. Schulz

CHALLENGE

The above book lays out a visual biography of Charles M. Schulz, the creater of the "Peanuts" comics. I researched and designed the book, displaying his comics at large scale next to his life's story, making correlations between the themes in his comics and Schulz's own life.

The book uses primary colors to highlight his natural, and simple drawings, and allow the complex themes and words of the comics speak for themselves. Additionally, the headlines are all set in the type he uses his comics.
