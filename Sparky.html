Project: Sparky: The Life and Times of Charles M. Schulz

The above book lays out a visual biography of Charles M. Schulz, the creater of the "Peanuts" comics. I researched and designed the book, displaying his comics at large scale next to his life's story, making correlations between the themes in his comics and Schulz's own life.

The book uses primary colors to highlight his minimalistic style, and allow for the complex themes and words of the comics to speak for themselves. Additionally, the chapter headlines are all set in the type used in his comics.
