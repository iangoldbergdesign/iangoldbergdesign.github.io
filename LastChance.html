WORK EXPERIENCE: MIG, Inc.

CLIENT: Caltrans

CHALLENGE

Caltrans, the California Department of Transportation, was interested in building a website to explain their strategy, and eventual solution for instability and roadway failure on a 4-mile segment of US Highway 101 in Del Norte County.

The primary goal of the website is for Caltrans to be transparent about the research they are performing, the roadway alternatives they were considering (executed through large diagrams and supplementary text), and for communicating a frequently updated process and schedule of their eventual plan.

This accessible and fully responsive website is being used along with community meetings and other outreach materials and events to educate, and gather community input.

Additionally, the decision to include a tab for users to sign up for email updates, and providing ample space on the homepage for Caltrans' Twitter feed, reinforces their transparency and community education.
