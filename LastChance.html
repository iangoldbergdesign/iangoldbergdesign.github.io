WORK EXPERIENCE: MIG, Inc.

CLIENT: Caltrans

CHALLENGE

Caltrans, the California Department of Transportation, was looking for a site to lay out the strategy and eventual solution for instability and roadway failure on a 4-mile segment of US Highway 101 in Del Norte County. The main mission of the site was for Caltrans to be transparent about the research they have performed, the alternatives they were weighing for the roadway, and assist them in communicating the process and schedule for the plan.

With this in mind, this accessible and fully responsive site focuses on the different aspects of the project that would be of interest to the community. Key elements include: highlighting the alternatives through large images and supplementary text, a document library, and a frequently updated "Process + Schedule" section. Additionally, the decision to include a tab for users to sign up for email updates, and giving Caltrans' Twitter feed space on the homepage, reinforces Caltrans transparency, and informs the community about the project.
