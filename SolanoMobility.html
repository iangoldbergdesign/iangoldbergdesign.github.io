WORK EXPERIENCE: MIG, Inc.

CLIENT: Solano Transportation Authority

CHALLENGE

The Solano Transportation Authority was looking for a website for Solano Mobility, a division geared towards seniors and people with disabilities. The website highlights the Mobility Call Center, as well as other programs like "Find a Ride" and their training videos.

An important aspect of this site is its accessibility. Given the audience, we made sure the text and navigation were extremely clear in both the language being used, and in their functionality. We also paid attention to making sure the contrast in colors was high for legibility, and the type size was large enough for readability.
