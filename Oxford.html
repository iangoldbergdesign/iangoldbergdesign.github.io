Project: Oxford University Press

At Oxford University Press, I had the opportunity to conceptualize and design book jackets for the Trade Art Department, three of which are shown above.

At the time of design, the final book text had not yet been completed, so through an outline and introductory content, as well as additional research on each book's theme, the jackets were developed.
