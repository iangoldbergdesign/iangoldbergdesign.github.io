Project: Ahead of the Curve Newsletter

Client: MIG, Inc.

MIG was looking to rethink the company e-newsletter, Ahead of the Curve. The newsletter is sent out quarterly to clients and professionals in the field. Each issue has a theme, showcasing both current projects, and the thinking taking place within MIG's offices. Collaborating with a writer and our Creative Director, I led the creative on the newsletter.

To better understand the user, I began by interviewing, and peforming paper prototype sessions with planners and community outreach specialists, two of the primary audiences for MIG's newsletters. This led to a better understanding of how users interact with the information as it enters into their email through MailChimp, and then onto the newsletter site.

Understanding that the primary reading experience would be on a user's mobile device, I approached the design mobile first. The look and feel applies the MIG brand, while also retaining a clean and minimal design to better highlight the project photos and article narratives, and create an easy user-experience.

Work completed at MIG, Inc.
