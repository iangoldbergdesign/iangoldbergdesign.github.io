Work Experience: MIG, Inc.

Client: Hayward Water Pollution Control Facility

Challenge

The above brochure was developed to educate Hayward residents on the water treatment process, and introduce them to the new treatment facility.

Through the use of bright colors, geometric shapes, as well the implementation of fresh and modern fonts, the design strives to engross readers into a subject that may lack excitement for most individuals, water treatment.

Specifically, we wanted to touch on the textures and shapes of the facility itself. The interwining tubes, the complexity of the digester, as well as the geometrical design of the solar panels are reflected in the dotted background pattern, the circular imagery, and the way the call-outs and highlighted text are treated. The slanted headlines are both playful and welcoming, as well as reference the angles and intersection of the different forms in the machine.
