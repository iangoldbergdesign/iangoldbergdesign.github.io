WORK EXPERIENCE: MIG, Inc.

CLIENT: Marin Clean Energy

CHALLENGE

This social marketing campaign was developed to change the behavior of Marin County residents, and save energy. When clicked, the ads led users to Marin Clean Energy's "My Energy" tool to help them calculate their energy costs.

Working with a writer, we developed a series of four animated ads featured on both local news sites, and a facebook graphic. The animations, each three frames long, use icons, simple imagery, and bright colors, in conjunction with the text, to reach viewers on a personal level.
