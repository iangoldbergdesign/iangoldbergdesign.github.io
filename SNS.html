Project: Outreach Pamphlet

Client: City of Henderson (Southern Nevada Strong)

The above pamphlet was developed to inform the Southern Nevada community of who Southern Nevada Strong is and how people can become involved in redevelopment and planning efforts in the region. The pamphlet was used along with community meetings and outreach efforts, led by MIG, to educate the community.

With the help of the City of Henderson's planners, in the center of the pamphlet I researched and designed a map of the region that shows the area's different districts. The design of the pamphlet takes cues from the organization's branding, which was previously developed.

Work completed at MIG, Inc.
